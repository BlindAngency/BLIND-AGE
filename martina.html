Martina Karny

Fashion stylist

Martina Karny, an award-winning stylist, fashionista, humanist, and artist, sought a comprehensive rebranding to reflect her unique style and cater to her target audience of individuals aged 40 and above. Our goal was to create a cohesive brand identity that embodied her personal ethos and professional expertise.

Brand Strategy:

We refined her brand name, created compelling taglines, and defined her Brand DNA to align all brand elements with her core values.

Visual Identity:

We designed a sophisticated logo with modern typography and artistic elements, reflecting Martina's creativity and professionalism.

Experience:

We created visually appealing social media content for platforms and a user-friendly, visually stunning website featuring her portfolio, client testimonials, a blog, and a booking system.

The rebranding of Martina Karny was a significant success, enhancing her professional image and expanding her reach. The new brand identity resonated well with her target audience, leading to increased engagement on social media and a rise in client inquiries. The cohesive and elegant brand presentation helped Martina establish herself as a leading image consultant for individuals over 40.
