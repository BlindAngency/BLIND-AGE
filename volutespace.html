Volute Space

Interior Design Studio

Volute Space, a forward-thinking interior design studio, sought to create a brand identity that would reflect their philosophy of "Spaces That Evolve." Our mission was to develop a comprehensive brand strategy that would resonate with their target audience and elevate their market presence.

Brand Strategy:

We named "Volute" to evoke elegance and fluidity, aligning with their design philosophy. We defined core values, mission, and vision for consistency.

Visual Identity:

We developed a sleek, modern logo with clean lines and a minimalist aesthetic. The color palette of black, white, and gray paired with elegant, modern fonts for a refined look.

Social Media:

We created visually appealing content for Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest to showcase projects and engage the audience.

User Experience:

We designed a visually stunning, user-friendly website featuring their portfolio and an easy-to-use inquiry system, ensuring a seamless brand experience.

We analyzed trends to make Volute Space's brand modern and appealing. The name "Volute Space" evokes elegance and fluidity. We defined their Brand DNA—core values, mission, and vision—for consistency. Our messaging, with the tagline "Spaces That Evolve," highlighted their expertise in creating serene, stylish interiors. We positioned Volute Space as a premier studio transforming spaces with timeless elegance, ensuring cohesive brand execution.
