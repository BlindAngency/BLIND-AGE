TEDx Event

TEDx Accra's "Agents of Change" event was a catalyst for transformation, designed to inspire and empower individuals to go beyond talking about change and become the change-makers themselves. Our mission was to craft a captivating and comprehensive brand identity from scratch, encompassing logo design, social media branding, dynamic content creation, eye-catching print design, an engaging website, and widespread publicity to amplify the event's impact.

Brand Strategy:

We aimed to create a dynamic identity that resonates with the audience, inspiring change and motivating actionable steps toward transformation.

Visual Identity:

We created a bold identity with a modern logo and red/black colors. Our striking designs for posters, banners, and merchandise ensured a cohesive visual experience.

Experience:

We used social media, a user-friendly website, and engaging content to highlight the event, inspire change, and encourage active participation.

The TEDx Accra "Agents of Change" event was a resounding success, with high engagement across all platforms. Our cohesive brand identity and engaging content created a memorable and impactful event, inspiring many to become agents of change. This case study exemplifies our commitment to comprehensive and impactful brand identities, showcasing our ability to deliver cohesive branding, compelling visuals, and engaging user experiences.
